About Aurum Comfort PG

Designed for comfort, safety and student living

Our Story

Aurum Comfort PG is a premium girls’ accommodation located in Vijay Nagar, just minutes away from DU North Campus. Designed keeping students in mind, it offers a perfect balance of comfort, safety, and convenience.

With hotel-like rooms, home-cooked meals, and a peaceful environment, we aim to provide a space where students can feel at home while focusing on their studies.

Our location near Hudson Lane ensures that everything you need — from cafes to daily essentials — is just a short walk away.
